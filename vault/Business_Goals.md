# Business Goals

## Primary Objectives

### 1. Increase Productivity
- **Target**: Reduce time spent on routine tasks by 70%
- **Metric**: Hours saved per week
- **Timeline**: Ongoing

### 2. Improve Task Management
- **Target**: Centralize all tasks in one system
- **Metric**: 100% of routine tasks captured and processed
- **Timeline**: Complete by end of Q1

### 3. Enhance Communication
- **Target**: Streamline email and message responses
- **Metric**: Response time reduction of 50%
- **Timeline**: Ongoing

### 4. Financial Oversight
- **Target**: Track expenses and identify cost savings
- **Metric**: Monthly cost reduction of 5%
- **Timeline**: Ongoing

### 5. Data Organization
- **Target**: Organize files and documents systematically
- **Metric**: 95% of files properly categorized
- **Timeline**: Complete by end of Q1

## Strategic Priorities

### Short-term (0-3 months)
